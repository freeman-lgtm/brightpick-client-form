Definitions

  • *Current Volumes Current average daily volumes at the site considered for fulfillment automation implementation.
  • *Design Period Volumes Estimated average daily volume, accounting for future requirements (usually 12–18 months) to be included in the design.
  • *SKUs A distinct item from your product catalogue. Example: an apparel SKU is unique at the style, colour, and size level.
  • *FC Fulfillment Centre.
  • *Product Catalog Product category, weight, dimensions, packaging, quantity in the package, division into classes, division into storage zones, and material.
  • *Database of Orders Ordered Date, Picked Date, Order ID, Order Lines ID, Picks.
